## Patterns in Your Taste

- **Visible computation** — mechanism IS the art, nothing hidden
- **Double representation** — same math shown two ways (Ramp: optical + physical, Shadow: gears above + light below, War-to-Peace: military + ethereal)
- **Quantized/discrete** over smooth — clicks, steps, terraces, prime numbers
- **Cross-century collisions** — Antikythera + Jantar Mantar, Curta + Leibniz, Naval WWII + tensegrity
- **Extremes, no middle** — ultra-flat (Ramp=30mm, Fabric=two sheets) AND dramatic vertical (Coaxial Screw elevator, Waffle Grid hanging blocks)
- **Aerospace grid frame** — precision engineering visible as ornament
- **Detailed non-simplified shapes** — you want to see every gear tooth, every linkage bar, every thread path
