                          WHAT CHANGED V56→V57
================================================================================

REMOVED:
  rice_tilt = 20 * sin(master_phase);  // ORPHAN ANIMATION

ADDED:
  rice_eccentric_phase = master_phase;
  rice_eccentric_offset = 10;
  rice_linkage_length = 30;
  rice_pin_y = rice_eccentric_offset * sin(rice_eccentric_phase);
  rice_tilt = asin(rice_pin_y / rice_linkage_length);

NEW MODULES:
  rice_eccentric_pin_assembly()
  rice_linkage_arm()
  rice_tube_single() (updated)

================================================================================
                          KINEMATIC EQUATIONS
================================================================================

INPUT: Master gear phase θ (0° to 360°)

ECCENTRIC PIN:
  x = 70 + 10*cos(θ)
  y = 30 + 10*sin(θ)

OUTPUT: RICE TUBE TILT
  rice_tilt = asin(10*sin(θ) / 30)

VERIFICATION:
  θ = 0°:    rice_tilt = 0°        ✓
  θ = 90°:   rice_tilt = 19.47°    ✓
  θ = 180°:  rice_tilt = 0°        ✓
