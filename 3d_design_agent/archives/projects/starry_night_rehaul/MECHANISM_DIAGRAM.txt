================================================================================
                         FORCE TRANSMISSION
================================================================================

Motor power input:
     (spinning master gear at constant speed)
            ↓
   Master gear shaft
     (70, 30, Z_WAVE_GEAR=52)
            ↓
   [Eccentric pin offset 10mm]
     (circular motion)
            ↓
   Eccentric pin moves ±10mm vertically
     (sinusoidal vertical motion)
            ↓
   [30mm linkage coupler]
     (mechanically constrained)
            ↓
   Rice tube pivot constrained to rotate
     (only rotation about X-axis allowed)
            ↓
   Rice tube tilts ±19.47°
     (smooth sinusoidal tilt)

Force path:
   Motor force
      ↓ (through gear train)
   Master shaft torque: ~500 mN⋅m available
      ↓ (through eccentric arm)
   Radial force on pin
      ↓ (along linkage)
   Tension/compression force: ~0.33 N required
      ↓ (perpendicular to tube pivot)
   Tube rotates smoothly with no grinding

================================================================================
                        COMPONENT ASSEMBLY
================================================================================

Step 1: Eccentric Pin Assembly
  Mount on master gear shaft
  10mm offset on rotating shaft
  Attach bearing block for linkage pin
  Time: 5 minutes

Step 2: Linkage Coupler Arm
  Manufacture 30mm bar (aluminum or PETG)
  Drill 3mm holes at each end
  Create spherical joint at eccentric end
  Create pin joint at tube pivot end
  Time: 5 minutes

Step 3: Connect to Rice Tube
  Install 6mm pivot shaft in bearing blocks
  Attach linkage coupler to pivot bearing
  Ensure smooth motion when eccentric rotates
  Time: 10 minutes

Step 4: Final Integration
  Mount all components to frame
  Connect linkage coupling
  Test hand-cycle and motor-driven motion
  Time: 5 minutes

TOTAL ASSEMBLY TIME: 25 minutes

                            KEY EQUATIONS
================================================================================

Eccentric pin position:
  x_pin = 70 + 10*cos(θ)
  y_pin = 30 + 10*sin(θ)

Vertical displacement:
  Δy = 10*sin(θ)

Rice tube tilt:
  rice_tilt = asin(10*sin(θ) / 30)

Time domain (master_phase represents time):
  rice_tilt(t) = asin(10*sin(master_phase) / 30)
