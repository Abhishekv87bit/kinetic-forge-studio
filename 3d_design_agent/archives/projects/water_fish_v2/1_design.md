# Design: Drip-Actuated Pendulum

## User Requirements
- Motion: Flowing/organic
- Feeling: Calm/meditative
- Complexity: Simple (1-2 parts)
- Power: Water drips (very low power)

## Mechanism Selected
**Drip-Actuated Pendulum**

## How It Works
1. Pendulum hangs at center balance
2. Water drips into cup on one side
3. Weight shifts, pendulum tips
4. Cup empties through drain hole
5. Pendulum swings back to center
6. Cycle repeats

## Why This Mechanism
- Drip power is sufficient (gravity assists)
- 1 moving part only
- Organic swinging motion
- Meditative rhythm
