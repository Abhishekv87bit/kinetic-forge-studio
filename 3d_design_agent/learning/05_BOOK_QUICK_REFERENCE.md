📖 **CRITICAL TABLE:** Making Things Move p.248, Table 8-1

This table answers: "How do I convert [this motion] to [that motion]?"

| FROM ↓ / TO → | Rotary | Oscillating | Linear | Reciprocating |

| **Rotary** | Gears | Crank-rocker | Rack & pinion | Crank-slider |
| **Oscillating** | Ratchet | Linkage | Cam | Linkage |
| **Linear** | Rack & pinion | Cam | Pulley | Cam |
| **Reciprocating** | Ratchet | Linkage | Direct | Cam |

## Book Strengths

### Robert Addams - "Automata Design"
✅ **Best for:** Visual diagrams, historical context, teaching children
✅ **Unique content:** Young children projects (p.64-69), drinks carton mechanisms
✅ **Style:** Hand-drawn diagrams, practical cardboard focus

### Making Things Move - Dustyn Roberts
✅ **Best for:** Engineering detail, modern applications, complete projects
✅ **Unique content:** Motion conversion table (p.248!), detailed calculations
✅ **Style:** Technical illustrations, step-by-step instructions

### Animal Joints Paper
✅ **Best for:** Bio-inspired design, understanding why linkage ratios work
✅ **Unique content:** Real animal measurements, motion amplification theory
✅ **Style:** Academic but accessible, excellent figures

### Making Automata (Scanned Images)
⚠️ **Note:** No text extraction possible - browse visually
✅ **Best for:** Inspiration, seeing finished automata
✅ **Style:** Photographs and illustrations
