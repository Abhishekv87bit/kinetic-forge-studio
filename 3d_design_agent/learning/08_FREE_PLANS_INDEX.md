## Tier 1: Essential Free PDFs

### Cabaret Mechanical Movement
**URL:** [CMU PDF](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/afs/cs/academic/class/15394h-f17/resources/cabaret_mechanical_movement.pdf)

**Content:**
- Mechanism theory from Cabaret Mechanical Theatre
- Over 200 B&W illustrations
- Basic mechanisms to construction tips
- Industry-standard reference

**Best for:** Understanding mechanism fundamentals

---

### Exploratorium Cardboard Automata
**URL:** [Exploratorium PDF](https://www.exploratorium.edu/pie/downloads/Cardboard_Automata.pdf)

**Content:**
- 18-page pamphlet
- Cams, levers, linkages explained
- Perfect for teaching your daughter!
- Step-by-step cardboard projects

**Best for:** Getting started with cardboard prototyping

## Download Workflow

1. **Start here:** Exploratorium PDF + Cabaret PDF
2. **For specific projects:** Dug North's index
3. **For laser cutting:** 3axis.co or Vectors File
4. **For advanced:** Clayton Boyer (paid)

---

## Remember

David C. Roy's advice: *"Start simple. Make a weight-driven escapement."*

Don't overwhelm yourself with complex plans. Start with:
1. Exploratorium cardboard automata
2. Simple cam toys
3. Basic four-bar linkages

Then progress to more complex designs.
