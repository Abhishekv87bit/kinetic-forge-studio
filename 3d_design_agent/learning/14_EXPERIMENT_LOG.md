# Signature Mechanism Experiment Log
## Tracking Your Journey to Find Your "Holy Numbers"

---

## How to Use This Log

For each experiment, record:
1. **What you tried** - The setup, parameters, domain
2. **What happened** - Observations, measurements
3. **How it felt** - Gut reaction, aesthetic response
4. **What you learned** - Insights for next experiment

The gut feeling is as important as the data. Jansen didn't find Strandbeest through pure math - he found it by noticing what fascinated him.

## Reflection Questions (Answer Periodically)

### Month 4:
- What surprised you during sampling?
- What domain keeps pulling you back?
- Are you fighting against any domain? Why?

### Month 8:
- Is the domain still exciting or becoming tedious?
- What aspect of the parameter space is most interesting?
- Have you found any "forbidden zones" that don't work?

### Month 12:
- Do the evolved parameters make intuitive sense?
- Would you have found these by hand?
- What did evolution teach you about the domain?

### Month 18:
- How has your aesthetic evolved?
- What would you tell your Month 1 self?
- Where will you take this mechanism next?
